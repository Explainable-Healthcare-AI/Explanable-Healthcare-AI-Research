Explainable AI for Heart Disease Prediction

 Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)-based healthcare platform capable of supporting transparent and trustworthy clinical decision support systems for heart disease prediction.

The system combines:
- Machine Learning
- SHAP Explainability
- Conversational Healthcare AI
- Clinical Reasoning Support
- Medical Knowledge Retrieval

to create interpretable and ethically governed healthcare AI systems.

##Problem Statement

Many healthcare AI systems operate as black-box models and fail to explain their predictions clearly to healthcare professionals. This lack of transparency reduces clinical trust and limits adoption in real healthcare environments.

This project aims to build:
- transparent AI systems
- explainable predictions
- trustworthy healthcare AI infrastructure
- ethical clinical decision support systems

##Core Features

- Heart Disease Prediction
- SHAP-based Explainability
- Conversational Clinical AI
- Medical Evidence Retrieval
- Clinical Decision Support
- Ethical Healthcare AI
